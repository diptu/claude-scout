## 🎯 Phase Responsibility
**Phase 1 — Business + Product Definition Layer**

This skill is responsible for translating CEO-level vision into:
- structured product requirements
- executable engineering scope
- prioritized delivery plan

---

# 🚀 Core Objective

To ensure the team builds the **right product**, not just a working product.

This skill acts as the **bridge between vision and execution**.

---

# 🧭 Primary Responsibilities

## 1. Vision Decomposition
Break CEO vision into:
- user problems
- business outcomes
- measurable goals

## 2. Requirement Engineering

- Constraints
